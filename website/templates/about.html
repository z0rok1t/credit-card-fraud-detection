ABOUT US


We are a team of students of INPT , working in the field of cybersecurity. Our focus is on applying techniques of artificial intelligence to help in detecting fraudulent transactions. Our work is based also on Machine learning to analyse data of a bank and provide the suspicious transactions in order to alert the bank and the owner of credit card.

With the increasing complexity of fraudulent activities, traditional rule-based systems often fall short in identifying new patterns and sophisticated fraud techniques. Our advanced machine learning algorithms continuously analyze vast amounts of transaction data, enabling us to adapt and stay ahead of evolving fraud tactics.
